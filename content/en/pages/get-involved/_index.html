If you would like to support the promotion of post-growth ideas and approaches in the Cologne-Bonn region by joining our group please contact us (contact form coming soon!) .
 
Our plenary meetings normally take place every second Monday, mostly in the city centre of Cologne. Smaller groups meet in-between to work on particular projects.
 
We are currently working on organising events like film screenings and visits to local post-growth-related initiatives. We also started building links to other local initiatives related to post-growth. We happily welcome people that would like to get involved in organising these events and activities. We are interested and open to hearing your ideas to promote post-growth, and we hope you can find like-minded people in our group to work together with.

Our events
Reading circle
One member suggests a short text such as an article or chapter of a book. Then we meet to share what stood out to us. Each person tends to bring their own experience and perspective and notices different things. This way we get to hear different views and get to know each other better.  To find out more - contact us (contact form coming soon)

Film screenings
Some of us are involved in a group planning an economics-themed film festival in Bonn in the Autumn.
 
To find out more - contact us (contact form coming soon)

Project visits
We are planning neighbourhood tours to visit projects that relate to post-growth ideas. We hope to run these in various neighbourhoods, where you can walk from one project to another and chat on the way.
 
To find out more - contact us (contact form coming soon)

Other events

Here we will link to events by other organisations, locally and online.
