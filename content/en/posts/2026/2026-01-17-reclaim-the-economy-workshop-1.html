Sunday 1st February 2026 3.45-6.15pm
What gives you hope for the future? Do you want to imagine an economy that puts people and planet first? Then come to our workshop, which is part of the global "Reclaim the Economy" week. In small groups (in English or German), we want to think about how we can succeed in making our society sustainable and socially just. You don't need to be an expert or have any prior knowledge. There will be coffee, tea and cookies :) Come at 3:45 pm, we start at 4 pm!   Kulturraum 405, Venloer Str 405, Ehrenfeld, Cologne.
